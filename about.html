About Me

Hello! My name is Anushka and I am a fanatic of everything vaporwave! I first got into this genre by watching fan-made "Simpsonwave" videos on Youtube, where creators would sample and distort early R&B 90s music and play it against scenes from the Simpsons. I love vaporwave as this art evokes feelings of nostalgia and calm happiness for me.
